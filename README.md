## ✨ Features

### 🎯 **Zero Installation Required**
Launch directly in your browser - no downloads, no setup, no hassle. Compatible with all modern browsers.

### 🎮 **Professional Training Modes**
- **Target Tracking**: Improve your tracking accuracy
- **Flick Shots**: Enhance your reaction speed
- **Precision Mode**: Master pinpoint accuracy
- **Speed Mode**: Train for quick target switching
- **Custom Scenarios**: Create your own training routines

### ⚙️ **Fully Customizable**
- Adjustable target sizes and speeds
- Custom crosshair designs
- Personalized difficulty settings
- Game-specific sensitivity matching

### 📊 **Performance Analytics**
- Real-time accuracy tracking
- Progress graphs and statistics
- Personal best records
- Detailed session breakdowns

### 🌐 **Multi-Language Support**
Available in English, Chinese, Japanese, Portuguese, Russian, Korean, German, and Spanish.

### 🆓 **100% Free Forever**
No hidden costs, no premium tiers, no ads interrupting your training.

## 🚀 Quick Start

Getting started with AimTrainerX is incredibly simple:

1. **Visit** [aimtrainerx.com](https://aimtrainerx.com)
2. **Choose** your training mode
3. **Configure** your settings (optional)
4. **Start** training immediately!

### First Time User?

1. Select your game from the dropdown menu
2. Enter your in-game sensitivity
3. Choose a beginner-friendly training mode
4. Click "Start Training"

**That's it!** You're now improving your aim.

## ❓ FAQ

### **Is AimTrainerX really free?**
Yes! 100% free with no hidden costs or premium features.

### **Do I need to download anything?**
No! AimTrainerX runs entirely in your web browser.

### **Which browsers are supported?**
All modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera.

### **Can I use it on mobile?**
Currently optimized for desktop. Mobile support is in development.

### **How do I import my game sensitivity?**
Select your game from the settings menu and enter your sensitivity value. We'll handle the conversion.

**More questions?** Check our [full FAQ](docs/FAQ.md) or [open an issue](https://github.com/LinightKira/aimtrainerx/issues/new).
